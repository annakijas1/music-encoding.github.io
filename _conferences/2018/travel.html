Welcome to College Park and the Washington DC area

Accommodation

College Park Marriott Hotel

Participants are strongly encouraged to stay at the College Park Marriott Hotel & Conference Center, at the negotiated rate of $179 per night (plus applicable taxes). This hotel is a 10 minute walk from The Clarice Smith Performing Arts Center (main venue). Use the “Book Hotel” button below to access the dedicated booking website to reserve a room at the group rate. If you prefer to reserve a room over the phone, please call +1 888.236.2427 and identify yourself as a participant of the “Music Encoding Conference” in order to receive the group rate. Group rates will be available for reservations from May 21 through May 25.

You must reserve your room no later than 30 April 2018 to receive the group rate. Free parking is available in the parking garage adjacent to the Marriott Inn and Conference Center.

Staying downtown in Washington DC
It is possible to stay downtown, but allow an hour door-to-door to get to and from the conference venue. We recommend the Chinatown area, with many hotel options, near all the major downtown sights, and on the Metro Green Line, which goes straight to College Park (see 'Maps and Dining'). Please write to conference2018@music-encoding.org if you have any questions.

Getting to College Park

Directions from Airports

The three major airports in the Washington area are Reagan Washington National (DCA), Baltimore-Washington International Thurgood Marshall (BWI), and Washington Dulles International (IAD).

The most convenient way of getting to your final destination is by using a door-to-door shared ride service such as SuperShuttle, which can be booked in advance. See below for public transport options.

From Reagan National Airport (DCA)
It is easiest to take the Metro to campus from Reagan National Airport. Follow the signs in the airport to the Metro station and take the Yellow Line north (towards Georgia Ave/Petworth). Get off at L’Enfant Plaza and change to the Green Line. Take the Green Line North towards Greenbelt and get off at the College Park/University of Maryland stop. Take the University of Maryland shuttle (104) to Cole Student Acitivties Building (last stop). This is at a short walk from the Marriott and the main venue.
From Baltimore Washington International (BWI)
Take the BWI Express Metro Bus from the airport to the Greenbelt station. Take the Green Line south one stop to the College Park/University of Maryland station. University of Maryland shuttle (104) to Cole Student Acitivties Building (last stop). This is at a short walk from the Marriott and the main venue.
From Washington Dulles Internation Airport (IAD)
Take the Washington Flyer from the airport to the West Falls Church Metro Station. Take the Orange Line east towards New Carrollton. Get off at L’Enfant Plaza and change to the Green Line. Take the Green Line North towards Greenbelt and get off at the College Park–University of Maryland stop. Take the University of Maryland shuttle (104) to Cole Student Acitivties Building (last stop). This is at a short walk from the Marriott and the main venue.
Directions from the Metro

The College Park Metro Station is located on River Road off Paint Branch Parkway, approximately two kilometers from campus. The College Park Metro Station UM Shuttle (104) leaves approximately every ten minutes or less from the UM Shuttle stop on the east side of the station. It will drop you off in front of the Cole Student Activities Building (#162 on the campus map). Walk to Clarice Smith Performing Arts Center (#386 on the campus map).

Parking

If you are staying within walking distance of campus, you may want to leave your car at the hotel and walk or take the hotel’s shuttle bus (if provided).

There are pay lots and garages throughout campus—these are run by automated pay stations, and they take either cash or credit. Rates are $3 per hour up to a $15 per day maximum. You can park in any of the highlighted garages/lots. You can find more parking information on the University’s Department of Transportation Services website.
