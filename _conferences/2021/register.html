Registration

IMPORTANT: Due to COVID-19 travel restrictions, most of the authors have confirmed that they will attend the conference online.

Please register on the ConfTool website: https://www.conftool.net/music-encoding2021/

The registration timelines and rates are as follows:

If you are a student, learn more about how you can apply for a bursary.

Early and late registrations include admission to all activities, including tutorials and main conference presentations, coffee for the tutorials, coffee and lunch breaks for the conference and the food event (dinner or lunch). On-site registrations are not guaranteed to have access to the food event.

Tutorials will be announced soon and a registration form will be made public.

All attendees are expected to adhere to the conference Code of Conduct.

Refund Policy

Once you’ve registered, you’ll have access at least to the Online mode. Cancellations made before 1st July 2021 will receive a full refund minus the administration fee (15€).
