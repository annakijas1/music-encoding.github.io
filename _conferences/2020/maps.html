Update: The Music Encoding Conference will no longer be held on site at Tufts University due to Covid-19.


Conference events will be held in Tisch Library and the Granoff Music Center on the Medford campus.

Tisch Library

The address of Tisch Library is 35 Professors Row, Medford, MA 02155. It is a 5-10 minute walk up the hill from Granoff Music Center.

Distler Hall and Granoff Music Center

The address of Granoff Music Center is 20 Talbot Ave, Medford, MA 02155. It is a 5-10 minute walk down the hill from Tisch Library.
