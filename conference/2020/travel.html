Update: The Music Encoding Conference will no longer be held on site at Tufts University due to Covid-19.


The conference will be held on the Medford campus located at 419 Boston Avenue, Medford, Massachusetts 02155.

Directions from Airport to Medford area

For directions from the airport and other locations, as well as parking and public transportation information, please visit https://as.tufts.edu/music/about/directions.

Please note that if you will be traveling from Boston Logan International Airport to Medford, MA, you can take the FREE Silver Line inbound to South Station with a free transfer to the Red Line all the way to Davis Square.

Traveling While Here

If you are new to the area, take a look at this helpful guide about navigating the public transportation (aka "T") in the Boston area. Lyft and Uber are also great ways to get around the area!

Depending on where you choose to stay, there are various ways to get to Tufts via public transportation. Davis Square is the closest MBTA train stop, it is on the Red Line. There are also many bus lines on the MBTA as well, and you may choose to take a bus from Davis to Tufts if you don't want to walk. If you are staying at a local hotel you can inquire about free shuttle services between the hotel and campus.

Accommodations

  

The Hyatt Place Boston/Medford (hotel website) is about a 1.4 mile walk to the Granoff Music Center. The hotel offers a free shuttle service.

  • The room block rate for the MEC conference: $190/King Bed.
  • Reservations should be made by individuals before Friday, May 1, 2020 by Calling 1-888 HYATT HP (1-888-492-8847) or by going online to Hyatt.com and entering reservation using G-ME20 as the group/corporate number (Reservation requests received after the cut-off date will be based on availability at the Hotel’s prevailing rates).
