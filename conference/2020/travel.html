Welcome to Medford and Tufts University

The conference will be held on the Medford campus.

Directions from Airport to Medford area

For directions from the airport and other locations, as well as parking and public transportation information, please visit https://as.tufts.edu/music/about/directions

Please note that if you will be traveling from Boston Logan International Airport to Medford, MA, you can take the FREE Silver Line inbound to South Station with a free transfer to the Red Line all the way to Davis Square.

Traveling While Here

If you are new to the area, take a look at this helpful guide about navigating the public transportation (aka "T") in the Boston area. Lyft and Uber are also great ways to get around the area!

Depending on where you choose to stay, there are various ways to get to Tufts via public transportation. Davis Square is the closest MBTA train stop, it's on the Red Line. There are also many bus lines on the MBTA as well, and you may choose to take a bus from Davis to Tufts if you don't want to walk.
